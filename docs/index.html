What it does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

01
📁

Folder Organization

Build your library your way. Custom folders, your structure. No algorithm deciding what you see.

02
⬇️

Built-in Downloader

yt-dlp powered with a full queue system. Line up downloads and let them run in the background.

03
🎵

Proper MP3 Metadata

Thumbnail scraped and embedded as album art. Artist and title embedded as ID3 tags. Every player shows it correctly.

04
📦

Import / Export

Export any folder as a ZIP and share it. Anyone with LinkVault can import your full collection instantly.

05
🔍

Cross-Folder Search

Search by artist or song name from the main screen. Find anything across all folders instantly.

06
🔒

Fully Private

No account. No analytics. No telemetry. Everything stays on your device. Always.
